About Atder

    Atder is an art platform dedicated to providing a vibrant and supportive community for artists and illustrators worldwide. Our platform serves as a hub where creators of all levels can showcase their talent, connect with fellow artists, and explore a diverse range of artistic styles.

    At Atder, we believe that art has the power to inspire, unite, and enrich lives. Whether you're an emerging artist looking to share your work with the world or an established professional seeking new opportunities for collaboration and growth, Atder offers a welcoming space for you to thrive.

    Atder is more than just a platform for showcasing artwork; it's a vibrant community where artists can find inspiration, support, and camaraderie. We encourage constructive feedback, collaboration, and dialogue among our members, fostering a culture of growth and mutual respect.

    Join us at Atder and become part of a dynamic community of artists who are passionate about sharing their creativity and making a positive impact through art. Whether you're here to explore, create, or connect, Atder welcomes you with open arms.

Rules

  1. Original Content: Only share original artwork created by yourself. Plagiarism is not tolerated.
  2. Respect Copyright: Ensure you have the right to share any artwork or content you post.
  3. No AI-generated Art: Art generated by artificial intelligence algorithms is not permitted.
  4. No Hate Speech: Any form of hate speech is strictly prohibited.
  5. Follow Guidelines: Adhere to any specific guidelines or rules set by the community moderators.
  6. Be Supportive: Encourage and support fellow artists; create a positive environment.
